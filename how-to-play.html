Cards Game

How to Play


Thanks for choosing our Cards Game©.




This is a breif guide of how to play our lovely game.


◦ Main Game◦

‣ Level: Shows your current level.
‣ Timer: Shows a timer starting from your first click/ level.
‣ Clicks: Shows your remaining clicks. You lose when you have no remaining clicks.
‣ Score: Shows your current score.
‣ Reset: To reset your game and start level 1.


◦ Gameplay◦

‣ Cards are shuffled each turn.
‣ Turn over any two cards.
‣ If the two cards match, they will be hidden.
‣ If they don't match, they will be turned back over.
‣ Remember what was on each card and where it was.
‣ The game is over when all the cards have been matched.
‣ End the game within a short time.
‣ The player with the most matches/ minimum time wins.
